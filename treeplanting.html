Ever Greening Program

Give and Grow community development Organization.( GAGCDO ) conducted its first international peace day celebration at MANDERA COUNTY- MANDERA NORTH-RHAMU TOWN through Environmental conservation under their ever greening project. The overall goal was to contribute to the fight against climate change which has just become the greatest challenge of survival for mankind. The program was meant to bring people together as a global community to join the fight against climate change to overcome the climate crisis such as poverty & hunger and caring for the land. Together we can mitigate the effects of climate change and improve the livelihood of the Rural communities & small holder farmers. Support us, support them, support all.
