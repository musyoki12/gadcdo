What We Do

Counter Violence and Extremism

The future of humanity is in the future of co-existence. Extremism is holding of an extreme political or religious view far outside the mainstream attitude of the society. Critically ,unemployment and poverty alone is not the only push factor rather the perception of injustice, human right violation ,social political exclusion widespread corruption or sustained mistreatment of certain group are also important push factors. When all this horizontal inequality come together for a particular group radical movement and element are more likely to erupt. The organization build resilience of venerable local community against negative and radical narrative. we work with academia to ensure that the research is grounded into the daily reality the community is facing. The organization is looking forward to engage media to encourage programming that foster tolerance and civic value.

Carping Illiteracy Rate (provision of adult education)

The type of adult learning and education activities vary widely. It include many learning opportunities for equipping adult with literacy and basic skills and for active citizenship through community popular or liberal education. This foster sustainable and inclusive economic growth . Its therefore a crucial tool in alleviating poverty, improving health and contributing to sustainable learning community. Human are inquisive and work better when not kept in darkness. Give and grow develop integrated policy using inter-sectrol knowledge for expertise encompassing education , training policy and related policy of justice, Agriculture and culture so as to
• Empower economically marginalized adult to understand question and transform through critical Awareness, the sources of their marginalization including lifting themselves out of poverty.
• Develop the capacity of individual to think critically and to act with autonomy and sense of responsibility.
• To reinforce capacity to deal with and shape the development taking place in the economy.
• To contribute to the creation of a learning society where every individual has an opportunity to learn and fully participate in a sustainable development process to enhance solidarity among people.
• To foster resilience in young and older adult.

Eradication of Poverty Through Agricultural Education

“We blame the well for been so deep when its actually our robe that is too short” The climate in the area is a semi arid and an average temperature of 26. The driest month is august with 0 precipitation and April has a high precipitation with a average of 86mm.A vital lifeline, river Daua flows 160KM along north and north east area of mandera which marks the boundary between Kenya and Ethiopia which help with water for irrigation to grow various crop like cowpeas, onion ,pawpaw, citrus, bananas and melons among others. The community are facing high reality that is exacerbated by climate change , poverty and conflict. Imagine a mother who toils whole day in the field but still goes to bed with stomach aching from hunger because she can not afford enough food to feed her family. There is another west African proverb that says “it’s a fool whose tomatoes are sold to him” unfortunately this is the reality in our community. But the true fool is the man who grows enough tomatoes to feed himself only to throw them away and buy someone else tomatoes. We can not continue to let this happen. Give and grown curb the high rate of dependency on the foreign aid through its objective
• Improve knowledge of diffusion process through the formation of famers association
• Ensure the knowledge generated are appropriate and derive technical recommendation
• Improvement of a rain-fed farming and family livestock rising.
• Silvo-pastrol development.

Who We Are

Give and grow is a community based organization comprise of university graduates and student determine to develop the society. As been known, mandera county is recognized for its under development , inter-clan conflict, higher rate of illiteracy and prejudice as ‘’terror zone’’. As an anti-automatons and a curios group who were determined to know the cause that drags the community behind we carried out investigation that comprise of questionnaires, pattern seeking , researching ,fair testing and some in-depth analysis which led to the formation of the C.B.O. the organization is based in mandera north and was providing service for the past one and a half years.. The mission of the organization are;
a) provision of mentorship session.
b) counter violence and extremism.
c) carping illiteracy (adult education).
d) Agricultural education.
e) Campaign against drug abuse.
